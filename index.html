Meet

Hi, I'm Dina Bond — a web designer and graduate of the Interactive Media Design program at Fanshawe College. I have 2 years of experience creating visuals, including images, covers, cards for social media, websites, and various things that you can see online.

In my free time, I enjoy watching movies, hiking, and making collages — a mix of getting lost in nature, lost in stories, and sometimes just lost in a pile of paper and glue.
