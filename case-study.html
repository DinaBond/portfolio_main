FANSHAWE HACKATHON

VIDEO ANIMATION

PROJECT OVERVIEW

For this project, I created a quick-animation video for Hackathon at Fanshawe college. The goal was to represent a totally immersive Interactive media design program and mention the Industry Night Event. The style of the final video aligned with college branding.

SUMMARY:

  • Nov. 2021
  • Role: Animation Designer, Editor
  • Deliverables: edited photos with smooth transition, app animations editing
  • Design tools: Figma, After Effects, Illustrator

PROBLEMS:

  • Packing wealth of information inside limited time frames
  • Make sure to both illustrate style and college mission
  • Using provided footage/assets versus story

RESEARCH:

To help me with building research archives, I search for modern and dynamic university videos. It helped me to find reference which I repeated to get the required screen motion.

PROCESS:

  • Script
  • Music
